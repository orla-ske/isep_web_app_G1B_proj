Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PetStride

PetStride is a platform that connects pet owners with trusted caregivers in their area. Whether you need a dog walker, pet sitter, or someone to care for your furry friend while you're away, we make it easy to find the perfect match.

Simply create an account, browse available caregivers in your area, check their profiles and reviews, and send a booking request. Once the caregiver accepts, you'll receive confirmation and can coordinate the details.

Yes, all caregivers on PetStride go through a verification process including background checks and reference verification. We prioritize the safety and well-being of your pets.

Our caregivers offer various services including dog walking, pet sitting, overnight care, drop-in visits, pet grooming, and training sessions. Each caregiver specifies their available services on their profile.

Caregivers set their own rates based on their experience, services offered, and market rates in their area. You can view pricing on each caregiver's profile before booking.

We understand plans change. Our cancellation policy allows free cancellation up to 48 hours before the scheduled service. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a partial fee.

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processing. Your payment information is never shared with caregivers and all transactions are handled securely through our platform.

Yes! Once you find a caregiver you and your pet love, you can book recurring appointments with them directly through the platform.

PetStride currently supports dogs, cats, birds, small mammals, reptiles, and fish. Caregivers specify which types of pets they're comfortable caring for on their profiles.

Click on 'Become a Caregiver' in the navigation menu, fill out the application form, complete the verification process, and once approved, you can start accepting bookings!
